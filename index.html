The Standard

Why Covet & Mane.

Brass-free. Silicone-free. Cuticle intact. Ethically sourced. The standard the industry's most coveted stylists, salons, and celebrity glam teams have chosen — because the hair, and the scalp it sits on, deserve better than coated.

01
Scalp Health, First.
100% silicone-free. Never coated. Built to install clean and wear comfortably day and night — without the buildup, dryness, or friction that compromises the scalp underneath.
02
Ethically Sourced.
Asian Remy and Indian Temple hair, virgin and cuticle-intact. Spot-checked at every stage. Our proprietary dye process delivers brass-free color that won't fade — and a supply chain we welcome you behind the curtain on.
