The Story

"A great install isn't a technique. It's a conversation between your hands and her hair."

Tori joined the Covet & Mane Educator team in 2022 after a decade in West Hollywood salons working on red-carpet hair and high-profile client transformations. Her specialty is The Keratin Tip — specifically the multi-tonal blending technique now taught across the Advanced certification.

She teaches the way she learned: hands-on, with real models, no shortcuts. She'll tell you when your sectioning is wrong. She'll also tell you exactly how to fix it.
