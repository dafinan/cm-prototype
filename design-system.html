02

Typography

Two families. EB Garamond italic is the stand-in for licensed Adobe Caslon Pro Italic — used for headlines, eyebrows of italic emphasis, and editorial pull quotes. Nunito Sans is the stand-in for licensed Avenir — used for body copy, nav, labels, and CTAs. When the production rebuild lands, swap the --font-serif and --font-sans declarations in tokens.css.

06

Editorial Prose

Covet & Mane is a luxury, invitation-only brand for stylists who are masters of their technique. From ethically sourced, double-drawn Remy hair to a 3-tier certification path, every part of the business is designed to set a higher standard — and hold it.

The Coveted is the consumer side of the same world: a directory of certified stylists, plus the discovery and aftercare content clients need to make extensions a sustainable part of their hair routine.

Caption / meta type — set in body-sm and muted by default. Used for timestamps, attributions, image captions, and footnotes.

07

Surfaces — Radius & Shadow

Soft --shadow-soft
Card --shadow-card
Lifted --shadow-lifted

Radius tokens are deliberately tight — luxury brand reads with sharper edges than rounded. --radius-sm = 2px, --radius-md = 4px, --radius-pill = 999px (badges only).
