Product Detail Page · Three Directions

Same product. Three different worlds.

The current covetandmane.com PDP is a transactional Shopify template — add-to-cart and out. For logged-in members of the Collective, this page should do more. Cross-sell into education, color, and tools. Showcase the work in real life. Embed the community proof so the page sells the system, not just the SKU.

Below are three different stances on how that integration should work, each with its own cart-drawer cross-sell pattern. Same demo product across all three — The Cut-Point Weft in Silky Straight.

How to choose

Three real choices, not three styles.

Axis · Buy vs Narrative

V1 = buy first. V2 = story first. V3 = system first. Pro stylists who already know the SKU prefer V1. New members or consumers (if we ever extended this) lean V2.

Axis · Cross-sell density

V1 light, V2 embedded, V3 aggressive. V3 makes the education sale most directly — aligns with "education is the gate." V2 frames cross-sell as editorial. V1 keeps it minimal.

Axis · Role of community

V1 community = proof. V2 community = voice in the story. V3 community = recommendations (what stylists like you bundled with this).

My gut says V3 hits closest to where you said this should go — the "education as the gate" frame argues for treating education on equal footing with product. But V1 is the fastest path and could ship in two weeks. V2 is the most differentiated brand-wise.
