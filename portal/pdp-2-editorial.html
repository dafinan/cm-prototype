01.
The Patent

Two cuts. No unravel.

Every Cut-Point Weft has two patented cut points along its length. Cut anywhere between them and the weft stays intact — no fraying, no unraveling, no fishline. Patented in 2021, after eighteen months of testing in working salons.

It's the reason a single weft can serve clients across three head shapes. It's also the reason your install lasts.
