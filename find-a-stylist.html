The Standard

Why a Coveted Stylist.

01

Certified.

Every stylist in this directory has completed Foundations — the Covet & Mane method, sourcing story, color theory, and clean installation principles. No exceptions, no shortcuts.

02

Premium Product.

C&M extensions are exclusive to licensed professionals. Silicone-free, brass-free, ethically sourced — the hair your stylist works with is the same hair seen on Zendaya, Zoë Saldaña, and Jessica Alba.

03

Accountable.

The Collective holds a standard. If something goes wrong with your install, your stylist has direct access to a C&M educator and a real human rep — and you get the result you came in for.

Behind the Chair

The extensions your stylist uses.

Ask your stylist which method is right for you — every C&M extension is available exclusively to licensed professionals.

Real C&M — The Coveted Tape
Tape-In · Quickest Install

The Coveted Tape

Undetectable, damage-free, and rapid to install. Tape-ins are a clean entry point — perfect for first-time extension wearers or anyone who wants minimum maintenance between visits.

Best for: fine to medium hair · 6–8 week refresh
Real C&M — The Cut-Point Weft
Hand-Tied · Most Versatile

Hand-Tied Wefts

Sewn under your natural hair onto small beaded rows. Lightweight, secure, and infinitely customizable. The go-to for natural movement and long-term wear.

Best for: medium to thick hair · 6–10 week refresh
Real C&M — The Keratin Tip
Keratin Tip · Most Seamless

The Keratin Tip

Pre-tipped, keratin-bonded extensions installed strand by strand. The most undetectable method — built for fine hair, face-framing detail work, and red-carpet finishes.

Best for: fine to medium hair · 8–12 week refresh
