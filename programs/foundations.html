What you'll learn.

01

The Covet & Mane Method

Why we exist, what's different about our wefts, what "the standard" actually means in practice. 24 min.

Sourcing & Quality

Asian Remy vs Indian Temple, double-drawn vs single, dye process, scalp safety. 32 min.

The Consultation Framework

How to read a head, set client expectations, price the install, and handle "I want it longer" with grace. 28 min.

Color Theory for Extensions

Reading undertones, blending across two textures, root-melt expectations, when to send your client home with a salon visit booked. 36 min.

The 7 install errors we see every single week, and the cleaner version of each. Cross-method foundations. 38 min.
